A Complete Guide: Travel the East Coast

Cairns

    Learn about Indigenous culture during the Cape Tribulation tour.
    Visit one of the seven wonders of the world at the Great Barrier Reef.
    Best time to go down the East Coast is September-October, while it is dry season in Cairns and warms up further down the coast.

Magnetic Island

    Visit the Koalas in the wild on the Fort Walk.
    Rent a roofless jeep so you can see the island in a day.
    Visit the rock wallabies at Geoffrey Bay Walk to the SS City of Adelaide.
    Go on an adventure to see the shipwreck around 11am when the tide is low.

Airlie Beach

    Sail the Whitsundays to see the Whitehaven Beach with powder white sand and stingrays close by.
    Snorkel in crystal clear water.
    Fly over Heart Reef on a helicopter ride to have the most scenic view of the Whitsundays.
    Go skydiving for the best adrenaline rush.

Noosa

    Visit Noosa National Park and enter the fairy pools where the water sparkles.
    Watch the sunset at Noosa River.

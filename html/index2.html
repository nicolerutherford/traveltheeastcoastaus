A Complete Guide: Travelling The East Coast

Brisbane

    See the Koalas and feed the kangaroos in a Lone Pine Sanctuary.
    Walk along to Kangaroo point and watch the sun go down on the industrial city.
    Appreciate beautiful artwork at the Queensland Art Gallery.
    Take a photo next to the iconic Brisbane sign.

Gold Coast

    Wear lots of suncream and visit Surfers Paradise Beach with the city skyline around.
    Enjoy lots of waterspouts, including jet skiing, wakeboarding, and kayaking with dolphins.
    Watch the sunrise in a hot air balloon.

Byron Bay

    Learn how to surf and take up surf camp with the professionals.
    Enjoy a beverage at the Stone & Wood Brewery then go sing your heart out at the piano bar.
    Venture out to Nimbin, a small hippie town.
    Get your steps in along the lighthouse trail and finish at Cape Byron Lighthouse.

Sydney

    Sydney is filled with many iconic sites to see such as the Opera House, the Harbour Bridge and the Botanical Gardens.
    Take the ferry across to Manly to enjoy the lovely beaches and chilled atmosphere.
    Enjoy dinner and drinks at The Rocks with a diverse range of restaurants to choose from.
    Follow the coastal walk to see the amazing coast line Sydney has to offer.
